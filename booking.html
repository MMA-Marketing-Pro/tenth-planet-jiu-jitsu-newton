Before You Arrive

What to bring

A top and pants (or athletic shorts) that are comfortable and easy to move in. A water bottle. That's it — we'll cover everything else on day one, including the waiver.

When You Get Here

First-class walk-through

Arrive 10 minutes early. Meet your coach, drop your things in the locker area, and either jump into class or watch from the side of the mat if that's more comfortable.

Where to Park

17 US-206, Augusta NJ

Free parking directly in front of the academy. Unit 1 is on the right as you pull in. Call (862) 258-8528 if you have trouble finding us.
