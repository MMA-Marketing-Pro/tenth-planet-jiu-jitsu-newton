10PMindfulness in Motion
The Philosophy

Martial arts is a way of life.

Learning a martial art can be the memorizing of moves. At 10th Planet Newton we focus on teaching our students much more than that.

When you drill a strike, a sweep, or a submission, the goal is to understand your body and be one with the movement. In time, you learn how to bring that amount of centeredness out exactly when you need it — and the stresses in your life become much more manageable.

That's the practice. That's the path. That's why we're here.

Learn technique. Find your center. Walk the path.

The 10th Planet Newton Way

The Instruction

Instructors who keep studying.

Our instructors are expertly trained martial artists and experienced teachers. They've earned their stripes — and they keep earning them, because no matter how much you've achieved, there's always more knowledge to gain.

Being able to impart knowledge isn't a simple process. It requires keen observation about individual students and how they absorb information, so we can make small adjustments in real time that unlock real progress. We're very proud of the programs we offer Newton, and we welcome you to try one to see why 10th Planet Newton is the right academy for you.
