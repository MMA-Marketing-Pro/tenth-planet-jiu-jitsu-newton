Why women's jiu-jitsu

Not a watered-down class. The same art.

This isn't "jiu-jitsu lite." It's the full 10th Planet system — rubber guard, lockdown, twister, every position in between — taught in a women-only space that takes the social pressure out of the room so the technique can take center stage.

Jiu-jitsu was built so that a smaller, lighter athlete can control a larger one. That principle does the most work for women. Leverage, timing, and angles beat raw strength every time — and that's exactly what we drill.

The Community

A room full of women who train.

There's something different about walking into a room where every other person on the mat is going through the same thing you are. The intimidation drops. The questions come easier. The reps stack up faster. That's what this class is for.

You'll meet beginners taking their first class, hobbyists with a couple stripes, and competitors prepping for tournaments — all on the same mat, all training together. It's a community first, a class second.
